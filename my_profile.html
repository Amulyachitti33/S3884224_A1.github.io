Project Idea

Overview:

My project idea is an application for the mental well-being of people. This app is for people who can't afford face-to-face theraphy, and hence, they can have online sessions. The AI assistant will guide them and look after their mental health. They can have multiple sessions but have to book appointments ahead. In this applicaton, we can record data, including health conditions, food diets, and sleep patterns. It also records the progress of the patient. For emergency purposes, that is, having a panic attack,anxiety outbreak, etc., they can double-click the volume up button, and we will send emergency support.

Motivation:

My whole idea behind the application is that everyone needs theraphy irrespective of how deep their pain is, but many people cna't afford it as it is expensive or time-consuming; travelling time. And few of them are scared to come forward and take theraphy. So this online theraphy is affordable; to be more precise, they can have unlimited access at a low price and have access to diets, meditations, exercises, etc. And it secures all their data. They can have multiple online sessions and always seek help. Anyone suffering from anxiety, depression, OCD, bipolar disorder, post-traumatic stress disorder, and other mental illness can use the application. It also suggests nearby hospitals for emergency or serious medical attention.

I strive that this will help people and maybe save a few lives.

Description:

In this application, there are various features. First, you have to sign up and mention your details and medical problems. You have to use your facial recognition to confirm and proceed and secure your data. When you put your data, you can book appointments with AI assistance. AI has speech recognition to know your emotions. So, during your online therapy, AI will recognize your speech and make notes of your mental health. Later after the session, it will send you the required food diets, exercises, and medications. A few activities include meditation, yoga, running/walking, and other activities. The application will also report your progress. You can download or watch food recipes and exercises. It also detects your sleep patterns, blood pressure, and heart rate.

If you have a sudden panic or anxiety attack, you can double press the volume up button for emergency. It alarms the nearby hospitals or clinics for emergency support.

You can also have multiple therapy sessions, twice or thrice a month, or even more.

You can view your previous records, which are available in the records menu. When you open the app, first, it asks for facial recognition, and then on the screen, there are a few tools available; to the right side of the screen, there is a menu bar(three horizontal lines), which includes your profile details, records, help, therapy, and a sign-out option. At the bottom of the screen, there are a few icons like food icons, exercise, sleep patterns, blood pressure, and heart rate icons. If you click each hero, it shows videos of food and activities or reports of your medical health.

You have another feature called SOS. When emergency, double click the volume up button to seek assistance. To start the therapy session, go to the menu, click on therapy, select the reason and click start. It takes you to a video conference panel.

There are two types of subscriptions; free and paid. There are a few benefits for a paid subscription like unlimited therapy sessions, food recipes, exercises videos, regular reviews on sleep patterns, blood pressure, heart rate. And other features like voice recognition, an emergency button is included. However, for a free subscription, voice recognition, food recipes, exercise, and analysis of sleep patterns are unavailable. And also, you will have only one therapy session every month.

These are some of the functional features available on the application.

The other essential skills required are understanding UX and UI designs. It will help if you understand user needs and preferences. The application should be user-friendly and straightforward. And always take feedback to work on unsuccessful points. You should also have experience with programming languages like Java, Swift, Python. Cross-platform app development is another skill needed; your app should work on all platforms like android, iOS, and windows. A business capability is another skill that is required. You need skills not just to make an application but also to sell it. And at last, always be aware of cybercrime. So, having knowledge about cybersecurity is a great skill.

This application will help a lot of people with their mental health. Mental health is as important as physical health, and people should come forward and seeek help. Low-cost online sessions and day-to- day observations will significnatly improve people's lives.
